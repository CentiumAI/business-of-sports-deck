04  ·  Vision  ·  Stadium to Summit
— Vision / We Are Creating This Program for Three Reasons

Stadium to Summit.

A path for students. A lens for business. A catalyst for the community of sport. Built inside the David Eccles School of Business and accountable to all three.

Three / Reasons
Reason 01

A career path in sport.

Open every door in the industry — front office, brand house, venue floor. Graduates leave with a vetted résumé, a capstone in the field, and a network of Utah's founding industry advisors behind them.

Reason 02

Business taught through the lens of sport.

Analytics, brand, and operations — taught the way the industry actually runs them. Every course is grounded in real teams, real briefs, and three integrated themes: AI, fan engagement, and sales strategy.

Reason 03

A catalyst for the community of sport.

Salt Lake hosts the 2034 Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games. The Jazz, RSL, Smith Entertainment, Miller, and Ski Utah are already at the table. The minor is how Utah supplies the talent the moment requires.
